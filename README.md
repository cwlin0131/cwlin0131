# CW (Chi Wei) Lin

Hi, I'm CW.

I've been building companies for over a decade. Before Portaly, I worked across education and gaming, partnering with organizations like the British Museum and Taiwan's Ministry of Culture, raising over USD $1M through crowdfunding along the way, and keynoting at SPIEL Essen — the world's largest board game fair.

Today, I'm fully focused on **Portaly**: a platform that helps creators turn social influence into sustainable income. Since 2022, we've grown to serve **200,000+ registered creators**, with 30% of our traffic coming from outside Taiwan — primarily Hong Kong, the US, Japan, and Indonesia. Portaly won **Product of the Day on Product Hunt (2023)** and was selected as one of **Taiwan's Top 30 Startups** by Meet Taipei the same year. In 2024, we 12x'd our revenue.

### What I spend my time on

Building Portaly means obsessing over two things:

- **The creator.** Over the past three years, I've personally run **300+ one-on-one interviews** with social managers, creators, and million-follower influencers — and coached dozens of them to generate millions of NTD in digital product revenue. Product decisions start here, not in a spreadsheet.
- **The craft of building.** I care deeply about how companies grow — PLG, growth hacking, and lately, building an AI-driven culture inside the team. I don't code, but I live inside AI tool chains daily, and I believe the next generation of product teams will be defined by how fluently they operate with AI.

### Where we're headed

The vision is simple: **help creators succeed.**

We're building the commerce infrastructure between creators and brands — the layer that lets anyone with an audience turn it into a business. Long term, we want to be the foundation of the global creator economy.
